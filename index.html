The Essential Itinerary

Highlights from an incredible journey exploring the rich history, vibrant cultures, and stunning architecture.

01

Views of Lisbon & Belém Tower

Lisbon, Portugal

Panoramic views over the hills of Lisbon

The 16th-century Belém Tower

The "glorious Eden" of Sintra

Started the morning at the top of Edward VII's Park for breathtaking panoramic views over the hills of Lisbon and the River Tagus. Later, we visited the marina in Belém to see the striking 16th-century Belém Tower and the Monument of Discoveries, tracing the steps of Portugal's great explorers. In the afternoon, we took a short excursion to Sintra, a lush and romantic retreat filled with lavish castles and historic tiles. The sheer beauty of the Sintra Royal Palace was unforgettable.

02

Discovering Seville & Flamenco Nights

Seville, Spain

The spectacular Plaza de España

The imposing Cathedral of Seville

After crossing the Spanish border into Andalucia, we spent a full day exploring Seville. We started at the breathtaking Plaza de España and wandered through the beautiful Maria Luisa Park. Walking through the narrow, quaint streets of the Barrio Santa Cruz was like stepping back in time. We also viewed the massive Cathedral of Seville, the resting place of Christopher Columbus. The night was capped off with a passionate traditional Flamenco show—experiencing the "deep song" and intense emotion in the birthplace of Flamenco.

03

The Mezquita of Cordoba & Madrid Bound

Cordoba & Madrid, Spain

The iconic arches of the Mezquita

Arriving in grand Madrid

Essential evening tapas

Departing Seville, we headed north toward Madrid with a highly anticipated stop in Cordoba. The absolute highlight was the Mezquita de Cordoba, a soaring cathedral transformed from a grand mosque. Walking through its iconic red-and-white striped arches was a surreal architectural experience. After taking in the sights of Cordoba, we continued our drive and arrived in the bustling capital of Madrid, finishing off the day with a spread of traditional Spanish tapas in the city center.
